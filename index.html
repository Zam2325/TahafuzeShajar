About us

At Tahafuz e Shajar, we believe that protecting trees is protecting life. Our mission is to reconnect people with nature by making tree care personal, meaningful, and accessible. Through our digital platform, we invite individuals to adopt a tree, give it a name, and become its guardian. Together, we are building a greener future— one tree at a time.

Trees of Islamabad

Shesham (Dalbergia Sissoo)

Info: A fast-growing tree known for its durable wood and shade.

Care: Requires full sun, well-drained soil, and occasional pruning.

Kachnar

Info: Ornamental tree with beautiful pink or white flowers.

Care: Prefers sunny locations and moderate watering.

Chir Pine

Info: Tall conifer found in hilly areas, with needle-like leaves.

Care: Needs full sun and tolerates poor soil conditions.

Neem

Info: Medicinal tree with antibacterial properties.

Care: Requires hot climates and little water once established.

Amaltas

Info: Known for its cascading yellow flowers.

Care: Grows well in full sunlight and well-drained soil.

Jacaranda

Info: Tropical tree with stunning purple blossoms.

Care: Needs warmth, full sunlight, and moist soil.

Kikar

Info: Thorny tree common in dry regions with yellow flowers.

Care: Thrives in dry climates, minimal watering required.

Deodar

Info: Himalayan cedar with graceful evergreen branches.

Care: Prefers cooler climates and well-drained soil.

Paper Mulbury

Info: Fast-growing tree used in handmade paper production.

Care: Requires moderate watering and sunlight.
